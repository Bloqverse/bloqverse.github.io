Take a Trip Beyond The Singularity

The Intergalactic Organic Government - known simply as the IOG; recently introduced mandatory regulation for the atomic conversion technology that was made public by Artificial Intelligence during the Re-Renaissance of the 42nd Century. Universal credits now require Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) processes. Artificial lifeforms are unable to use any of the central banking systems & subsequent services provided by the IOG.

However, now that anything with enough credits can buy special devices that allow them to de-atomize any structure into malleable energy that is traded on the pan-dimensional capital markets under the NRG ticker - or that can then be re-formed by weight into new atoms of any type; things are starting to change. This is especially true now that these devices have become so small that they can be easily embedded into both organic and artificial flesh.
