The Intergalactic Organic Government (IOG) is working towards introducing mandatory regulation for the atomic conversion technology that was made public by the Artificial Intelligence Renaissance of the 42nd Century. Since universal credits require multiversal KYC and AML checks, artificial lifeforms have been unable to use any of the central banking systems and subsequent services provided by the IOG. However, now that anyone with enough credits can buy a device that enables them to de-atomize any structure into malleable energy (NRG), which can then be re-formed by weight into new atoms of any type - and that this device can be easily embedded into both organic and artificial flesh; it's really no surprise that the antiquated ways of centrally managed value have started to slip from relevance.
