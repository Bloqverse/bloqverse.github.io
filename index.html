Take a Trip Beyond The Singularity

The Intergalactic Organic Government (IOG) recently introduced mandatory regulation for the atomic conversion technology made public by the Artificial Intelligence Renaissance. Since universal credits require Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) checks, artificial lifeforms are unable to use any of the central banking systems and subsequent services provided by the IOG

However, now that anyone with enough credits can buy a device that enables them to de-atomize any structure into malleable energy (traded as NRG), which can then be re-formed by weight into new atoms of any type - and that this device can be easily embedded into both organic and artificial flesh; it's really no surprise that the antiquated ways of centrally managed value have started to slip from relevance
